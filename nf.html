Bangkok

Thailand, officially known as the Kingdom of Thailand, is a Southeast Asian nation located in the center of the Indochina peninsula. It spans diverse ecosystems, including hilly forests, fertile rice fields, and rugged coasts. The capital city is Bangkok, and Chiang Mai is an important educational and cultural center in the northern part of the country. .

Demography

Thailand is ranked third in Southeast Asia and twentieth worldwide in terms of population. The population density in Thailand is 137 per Km2 (354 people per mi2). The total land area is 510,890 Km2 (197,256 sq. miles). 51.1% of the population is urban (35,698,325 people in 2020). The median age in Thailand is 40.1 years.
